## Language-by-Language: Top Gaps

### French — PASS
No gaps. 37/37 constructs covered. 207 tip cards, 5,376 quiz steps, 556 lessons. Gold standard for grammar instruction depth.

### Spanish — PASS
No gaps. 34/34 constructs covered. 246 tip cards, 4,910 quiz steps, 92 units. Strong across all PCIC levels.

### Korean — PASS
No gaps. 42/42 constructs covered. 332 tip cards, 6,074 quiz steps (highest of any language), 524 lessons. Korean has the most thorough grammar instruction in the app, befitting its morphological complexity.

### Dutch — NEAR-PASS
2 constructs without dedicated tip cards (both have teach card coverage):
1. **Coordinating Conjunctions** (B1) — 4 teach cards, no explanatory tip
2. **Continuous Aspect: Bezig Met/Aan het** (B1) — 9 teach cards, no comparison tip

Fix: Add 2 tip cards. Low effort, high pedagogical value.

### Italian — PARTIAL FAIL
13 constructs without tip cards. Most are A1-B1 level, making this more critical:
- **A1 gaps**: Subject Pronouns, Definite/Indefinite Articles, Negation, Questions & Numbers
- **A2 gaps**: Passato Prossimo vs Imperfetto (comparison tip), Stare + Gerundio, Comparatives & Superlatives
- **B1 gaps**: Relative Pronouns che/cui, Passive Voice, Si Impersonale, Trapassato Prossimo
- **B2 gaps**: Fare + Infinitive, Gerundio & Infinitive, Trapassato Remoto, Nominalizzazione, Advanced Connectors, Indirect Speech, Advanced Pronoun Placement, Register Variation, Advanced Passive, Discourse Connectors

Note: 35 tip cards total for 35 constructs sounds balanced, but tip card topics skew heavily toward B2 subjunctive/conditional, neglecting A1-B1.

Fix: Add ~13 tip cards for missing A1-B1 constructs. Medium effort.

### Japanese — NEAR-PASS
1 critical gap + 6 partial-coverage constructs:
- **CRITICAL**: Relative Clauses (連体修飾) (B1) — 0 dedicated teach cards, 0 quiz steps. This is JLPT N4/N3 core.
- Partial: のに/ので (A2), Volitional Form ましょう (A2), ほしい (B1), ながら (B1)
- ている and たい are covered by 16 and 5 teach cards respectively (adequate without tip)

Fix: Add 1 lesson on relative clause (noun modification) structure. Medium effort.

### Chinese — PARTIAL FAIL
13 constructs without coverage. 2 critical A2 gaps:
- **CRITICAL**: 着(zhe) Ongoing State (A2) — only 1 quiz step, no teach card
- **CRITICAL**: 正在(zhèngzài) Progressive (A2) — only 1 quiz step, no teach card
- A2-B1 gaps: 给 as preposition/verb, 从...到..., Directional Complements
- B2 gaps: 一边...一边, Topic-Comment Structure, 难道 Rhetorical Questions, Reduplication, 由 Formal Passive, 以 Classical Prepositions, Pivotal Construction, Complex Complement Chains
- Also: 吗 yes/no questions and 是 sentences lack dedicated instruction (practice only)

Fix: Add 2-3 tip cards for A2 aspect markers. Low-medium effort. B2 gaps lower priority given "NEW" status.

### Portuguese — FAIL
15 constructs without tip instruction. Also the lowest quiz density of any language (712 steps, 102 lessons).
- **A1-A2 critical gaps**: Pronunciation/Nasal Vowels, Contractions do/da/no/na/ao, Questions, Demonstratives, Negation, Gerund -ndo vs a+Infinitive
- **B1 gaps**: Conjunctions mas/porém/todavia, Passive Voice
- **B2 gaps**: Present Perfect, Pluperfect, Relative Pronouns, Haver as Impersonal, Cleft Sentences É que, Complex Conjunctions embora/contudo, Reported Speech, Ficar expressions, Diminutives, Verbal Periphrases, Idioms

Fix: Portuguese needs significant expansion — new tip cards AND increased lesson/quiz count (712 quizzes for 50 constructs averages only 14 per construct). This is a "NEW" language (2026-04-17) that needs further development.

### Russian — FAIL
Russian has two failure modes:
1. **Instruction gaps**: 15 constructs without tip cards, including Cyrillic Alphabet (A1), which means no script teaching.
2. **Structural gaps**: 12 of 36 units are stubs (Units 25-36, all B2.1-B2.2). ALL B2 constructs effectively have zero quiz practice. Only 395 quiz steps total.

Critical missing instruction:
- Cyrillic Alphabet (A1) — no script unit at all
- Zero-copula "to be" (A1)
- Question Formation (A1)
- Этот/Эта demonstratives (A1)
- Modal Verbs: можно/нужно/должен (B1)
- Relative Pronoun который (B1)
- Participles and деепричастия (B2)
- Passive Voice (B2)

Fix: Russian needs stub units 25-36 developed before B2 PP57 can pass. A1 script introduction unit is also needed. High effort.

## Methodology Notes

- "Taught" = at least one tip card OR verb_table explaining the construct's rule. Teach cards that demonstrate without explaining are counted as "partial."
- "Practiced" = evidence of 3+ quiz steps (mc/fb/match/drag_fill) requiring application of the construct. Approximated by grepping for construct-specific terms in quiz step lines.
- "Recycled" = construct appears in quiz steps in 2+ distinct lessons/units.
- Grep approach is approximate. Some quiz steps test constructs implicitly (e.g., any sentence in German requires adjective agreement). The audit intentionally requires explicit construct-labelled coverage.
- French/Spanish/Dutch/Korean counts are high-confidence. Italian/Japanese/Chinese/Portuguese/Russian counts may undercount coverage by 5-10% due to grep limitations on non-Latin scripts.
