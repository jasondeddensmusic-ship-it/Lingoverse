## Structural Warning

Portuguese has only 712 quiz steps across 102 lessons — averaging just 7 quizzes per lesson and ~14 per grammar construct. This is the lowest quiz density of any completed language in the app. PP57's requirement of "3+ quiz steps per construct" is technically met in aggregate, but per-construct targeted practice is very thin for the 15+ B2 constructs.

Portuguese also has only 36 units (compared to French/Spanish/Dutch with 89-92 units), and the CAPLE framework has 50 constructs. This creates structural pressure.
