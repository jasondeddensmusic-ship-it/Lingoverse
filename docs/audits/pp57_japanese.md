## Gaps Summary

**1 critical gap:**

1. Relative Clauses (連体修飾) (B1) — CRITICAL. No dedicated teach card for noun modification structure found. 0 quiz steps mentioning relative clauses. This is one of the most important B1-B2 structures in Japanese (the pre-nominal modifier system where clauses attach before nouns). JLPT N4/N3 explicitly tests this. Verifying in the batch files: the structure appears in example sentences but is never explained as a construct.

**5 partial-coverage constructs:**

2. ている (Ongoing State) — 16 teach cards, no tip. Coverage adequate via examples.
3. たい (Want to) — 5 teach cards, no tip. Coverage adequate.
4. Volitional Form ましょう — 1 teach card only. Thin.
5. のに/ので (Reason & Contrast) — 3 teach cards only. Borderline: PP57 requires 3+ quiz steps.
6. ながら/うちに/あいだに (While) — 1 teach card for ながら; うちに and あいだに may not be explicitly taught.
7. ほしい & てほしい — 2 teach cards, no tip.
