## Framework Notes

- Dutch has 89 units: U1-30 are core communicative units; U31-89 are NT2 vocabulary expansion batches. PP58 only applies to U1-30 effectively.
- The weather function (A2.F9) is absent from the core 30 units — Dutch A1-A2 does not have a dedicated weather unit, unlike German (U11).
- Dutch grammar is taught very densely (Er-forms at B1, passive at B1.2) which pushes some communicative functions into grammar-mechanics units rather than scenario-based lessons.
- The grammar mock content issue (per project notes) does not affect PP58 mapping since the audit uses unit subs/lesson titles rather than grammar content.
