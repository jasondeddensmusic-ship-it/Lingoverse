## Executive Summary

The A1+A2 story arc is **structurally sound** with a strong emotional trajectory from disorientation to belonging. The core character relationships (Verumius-Hildi, Verumius-Lukas, Verumius-Yilmaz) are well-established. However, there are significant distribution problems (two units nearly devoid of story), inconsistent character voices, and several missed emotional beats from the episode-scene-breakdowns design document. The German is generally native-quality but has a few moments of textbook stiffness. The English translations are accurate but occasionally lose tone.

**Overall rating: 3.2/5** -- Good foundation, needs targeted surgery, not a rewrite.

## Conclusion

The story arc has a strong skeleton. The Verumius-Hildi relationship is genuinely compelling, the KD payoff in Unit 12 is excellent, and the emotional trajectory from bewilderment to belonging works. The core problem is distribution: Units 4, 5, and 10 are narrative voids that undermine the experience, and Units 2 and 11 are thinner than their rich source material deserves.

**Estimated work:** 35-45 new story cards (Tier 1+2), 7 revisions (Tier 3). This is approximately 2 focused writing sessions.

**Priority order:** U5 first (Lukas origin story, downstream impact), then U4 (supermarket comedy), then U10 (Deutsche Bahn), then U11 (Gluhwein climax), then revisions.
