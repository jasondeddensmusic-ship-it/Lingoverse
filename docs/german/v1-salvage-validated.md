## Key Finding: Phase 1 Massively Overcounted

Phase 1 reported ~1,042 salvageable items (~600 TAKE). After validators grepped V2, the real numbers:

| Category | Phase 1 Estimate | Validated Actual | Why |
|----------|-----------------|-----------------|-----|
| Teach card TAKE | ~300+ | **~25** | V2 already has most words |
| deepDive grafts | not counted | **~20** | The real prize |
| Verb table additions | ~15 | **~5** | V2 has most already |
| Quiz step TAKE | ~200+ | **~78** (pending PP8) | Many redundant with V2 |
| PP52 violations found | 0 | **6** | Pre-existing V2 bugs |

**Bottom line**: The salvage value is concentrated in deepDive grafts (enriching existing tips) and fixing PP52 violations, not in adding new content.
