## Art Direction Notes

### Scene Modes
- **Cinematic**: Full-width AI background, character sprites slide in, visual novel dialogue box at bottom. Used for story intro (Scene 1) and story climax (Scene 3).
- **Bubble**: Candy bubble chat format with character avatar sprites beside each bubble. Used for teaching (Scene 2) and resolution (Scene 4).

### Verumius Expression Set (8 expressions)
1. Happy (waving) 2. Confused (tilted head, hand on chin) 3. Thinking (eyes up-right, finger on temple) 4. Excited (fists up, sparkle eyes) 5. Nervous (sweat drop, worried smile) 6. Proud (arms crossed, confident smirk) 7. Surprised (wide eyes, open mouth) 8. Embarrassed (blushing, hand behind head)

### Supporting Character Expression Minimum
Each recurring character needs at least 4 expressions. See Character Expression Summary at end.

### Midjourney Style Lock
All scenes use `--sref` (style reference) locked to VerumLingua moodboard: candy-colored illustrations, stylized characters, warm glowing scenes, glossy game art, Studio Ghibli environments. Purple #7B5EE8 in small details, gold #E8960A in light accents. No people in background plates (characters added as sprites). 16:9 ratio for scenes, leave center-bottom clear for UI overlay.
